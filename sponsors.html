Company Sponsors

Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur is one of the leading centres for education in world and undoubtedly the most respected technical institution India. The research oriented philosophy of the college is equally instilled amongst the brightest mind in the country. As a consequence of the sheer hard work and passion of a group of dedicated students,robowars IIT Kanpur, the robotic hub of the institute has been established.

The Robowar team aims to provide an opportunity to pursue passion for robotics. Through this journey they learnt to tackle real life engineering problems and gain the requisite expertise to solve many difficulties that they might face in the field of mechatronics.

Not only are we involved in fabrication and machining of the parts, but also put in a lot of effort into research work for material selection, weapon and electronics design, design for optimal manufacturing and costing parameters.

The task of putting together a robot is of paramount altitude and cannot be scaled without the support of sponsors. Sponsorship is a crucial aspect of any teams success. It lets us live up to our potential without any compromises and aim to achieve better than the best. It includes travel as well as an in-kind donation to the team. Funding for fabrication, materials, supplies and team logistics will also be highly appreciated. At the same time, the endowment of service and parts will also be an influential role in the growth.

We strive for a stable and mutually beneficial relationship with our sponsors because we firmly believe that the knowledge and expertise gained from the industries are of paramount importance.

We are looking forward to your generous contribution and also invite you to join hands with Robowars IIT Kanpur for a captivating and fulfilling experience.

Promotion through Techkriti

Techkriti is the annual technical entrepreneurship festival of IIT Kanpur. It hosts some of the most famous exhibitions, workshops, and speakers like Pradip Sindhu(Founder Juniper Networks), Dr. Hamid Karzai(Ex-president of Afghanistan), Mr. Ashish Chauhan(MD and CEO, BSE), Rajat Sharma(Chairman and editor-in-chief, India tv) and many great personalities from India and abroad.

Techkriti attracts students from over 150 colleges across the world.Sponsors receive widespread publicity through media posters reaching more than 800 colleges.
